Grilled Cheese Sticks



Ingredients

  • 125g Cheese
  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 60g of butter

Steps

  1. Cut cheese into slices
  2. Butter both slices of bread
  3. Place cheese on bread
  4. Join both pieces of bread
  5. Place under grill or sandwich press for 3-5 minutes or until golden brown and cheese has melted
  6. Enjoy your tasty, delicious snack
